Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how calories-macros.com ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

The calculator on this site runs entirely in your browser. Data you enter (age, weight, height, etc.) is never sent to our servers and is not stored or transmitted anywhere.

We may collect limited, non-personal technical data through analytics tools, including:

  • Pages visited and time on site
  • General geographic region (country/city level)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Referring website

This data is aggregated and anonymous — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use the site (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Advertising: If Google AdSense ads are displayed, Google may set cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

3. Google AdSense

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on users' prior visits. For more information visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. Third-Party Links

Our blog may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

5. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Use our contact form.
