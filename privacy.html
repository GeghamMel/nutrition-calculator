Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how calories-macros.com ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

The calculator on this site runs entirely in your browser. Data you enter (age, weight, height, etc.) is never sent to our servers and is not stored or transmitted anywhere.

We may collect limited, non-personal technical data through analytics tools, including:

  • Pages visited and time on site

This data is aggregated and anonymous — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use the site (e.g. Google Analytics)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

We only recommend products or services we believe may be genuinely useful, and an affiliate relationship never determines the factual content of our calculators, tables, or educational articles.

5. Children's Privacy
