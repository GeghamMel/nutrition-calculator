By trimester

Calorie needs during pregnancy

1

1st Trimester: +0–100 kcal

Calorie needs barely increase. Focus on folate, iron, and iodine.

2

2nd Trimester: +300 kcal

300 extra kcal/day. The baby is growing bones, muscles and organs rapidly.

3

3rd Trimester: +450 kcal

450 extra kcal/day. Baby gains most weight in the third trimester.

4

Breastfeeding: +500 kcal

Breastfeeding needs an extra 400–500 kcal/day above pre-pregnancy levels.
