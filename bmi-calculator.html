BMI categories explained

＜18.5

Underweight

May indicate nutritional deficiency. Consider consulting a doctor.

18.5–24.9

Normal weight

Associated with the lowest health risks for most adults.

25–29.9

Overweight

Slightly elevated health risk. Diet and exercise are typically recommended.

≥30

Obese

Associated with increased risk of heart disease, diabetes, and other conditions.
