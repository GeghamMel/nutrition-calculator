Body fat ranges

What is a healthy body fat percentage?

🏅

Essential fat

Men: 2–5% | Women: 10–13%. Minimum for basic physiological functions.

🏃

Athletic

Men: 6–13% | Women: 14–20%. Typical for athletes and highly active individuals.

✅

Fitness

Men: 14–17% | Women: 21–24%. Good fitness level for most adults.

⚠️

Overweight / obese

Men: 25%+ | Women: 32%+. Elevated health risks.
