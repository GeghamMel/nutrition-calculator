How it works

The science behind the calculation

1

MET value

Each activity has a MET value — a measure of how intense it is relative to rest.

2

Your weight

Heavier people burn more calories doing the same activity.

3

Duration

The formula is: Calories = MET × weight (kg) × duration (hours).
