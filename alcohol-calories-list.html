How alcohol affects weight and fitness

1

Empty calories with no nutrition

Alcohol has 7 kcal/g but provides zero nutritional value.

2

Alcohol slows fat burning

Fat burning is suppressed by up to 73% while alcohol is metabolised.

3

Reduces testosterone and recovery

Even moderate drinking reduces testosterone and impairs muscle recovery for 24+ hours.

4

Lower calorie options exist

Dry wine, spirits with soda, and light beer are far lower calorie than cocktails.
