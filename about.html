About

Calories & Macros is a simple calculator that helps you estimate daily calories and macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat). It’s designed to be fast, clear, and easy to use on mobile.

What the calculator provides

  • Estimated daily calories (maintenance / goal-based, depending on your inputs)
  • Suggested macro split (protein, carbs, fat)
  • Quick guidance on how to use the result

Important note

This website provides educational estimates only. It is not medical advice. For health conditions or specific dietary plans, consult a qualified professional.
