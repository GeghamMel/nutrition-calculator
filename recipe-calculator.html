How it works

Calculate any recipe in 3 steps

1

Search ingredients

Type the name of any food and select it from over 100 common ingredients in our database, spanning meat, dairy, grains, vegetables, fruits, and pantry staples. Each addition is tracked separately so you can review or remove individual ingredients before finalising the recipe.

2

Enter weight in grams

Weigh your ingredients on a kitchen scale for the most accurate results — this matters more than it might seem, since eyeballing portions is one of the biggest sources of error in home nutrition tracking. A basic digital kitchen scale costs very little and pays for itself quickly in tracking accuracy.

3

Get instant results

See calories and macros for the whole recipe and per serving automatically, based on how many servings you specify. This makes it easy to compare how a recipe fits into a daily calorie or protein target, or to scale a recipe up or down while keeping the per-serving numbers accurate.
